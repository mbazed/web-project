Your travel buddy for weather updates

To travel is to live. We all love to travel, to collect moments and to explore the world. Often we find ourselves in burdensome situations, just due to the weather conditions. How sad is it to place and not be able to explore your destination due to difficult weather conditions. So, here we are with a quick fix to this issue. Weather Companion is a web application specially designed to be your travel buddy. One can easily figure out the weather conditions at any selected location. We also suggests the ideal clothing, food and precautionary methods one has to take during the journey.
